About Artventures





THE CLUB YOU NEED FOR YOUR ART JOURNEY



♡ Our Mission ♡


Artventures is a newly founded art club (year 2024) that has the goal to make art easily accessible to students to explore art. We provide a safe and active community for art enthusiast students of Rizal Technological University.



♡ Our Motto ♡


"Art is not an inborn talent but a skill that needs practice."



♡ What we do ♡


A monthly art activity will be held at the university. Each month will feature a fun crafting project that will surely be enjoyed by all club members.
